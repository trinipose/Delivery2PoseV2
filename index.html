Strategic Location

Centrally located in Europe, reachable via a short flight from both Eastern and Western Europe. You can easily reach Italy by car, plane, train, boat or for the most adventurous, even bike!

For International flights, the Roma Fuimicino Airport (FCO) and Milano Malpensa Airport (MXP) host flights incoming from all around the world!

Experience Culinary Magic

Enjoy one of the best cuisines in the world...
Indulge in some of the world's favorite cocktails like Aperol Spritz, Negroni and Bellini.

For food, the options are endless: Pasta, Pizza, Risotto, Piadina, Gelato, Tiramisu and so much more! Get ready to experience food like nowhere else in the world!

Shop Local or Luxury

Wether it's Gucci or Versace, or a local boutique, enjoy the latest fashion, genuine silver products and beautiful craftmanship that Italy has to offer. If you're an non-EU resident, you can also shop Tax Free!
